Target Audience

The target audience will be business leaders and professionals who either are currently enagaged or wish to become engaged in business. These business leaders and professionals will be interested not only in the growth of their businesses but also in the growth and development of their communities. They'll be looking for a network to connect with other business leaders and professionals with the same goals and desires which Tip of the Iceberg will provide.

Personas

  • Scott is a young businessman who has just opened his own department store. His store is mildly successful and he is looking for advice on how to grow and expand his business. He hopes that by connecting with other store owners, he will get the invaluable advice that he needs to drive his business forward.
  • Laura is currently in her last year of high school and is considering what career she would like to enter when she finishes school. She is interested in pursuing business as her career but wishes to find out more about the field before making a decision. She thinks that by meeting others who have expereince in that field will help her make an informed decision about her future.

Scenarios

  • A businessman could be looking for information about a meeting of other businessmen in a similar field to his own that he wishes to attend.
  • A businesswoman could be looking to join her local trade guild.
  • A student who is interested in studying business could be looking for more information about his prospective career.
  • A businessman could be looking to start a commitee concerned with local business growth and development.
  • A businesswoman could be looking to connect and network with other business professionals in her field.
